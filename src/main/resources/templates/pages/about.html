About autodone

autodone is a service for the automated, time-controlled publication of status updates on any Mastodon instance. The codebase is developed under a free license by the Department of Digital Humanities at the University of Cologne and is open to all interested users.

Special features of the service include the ability to upload content in tabular format (tsv files) and the ability to publish posts as a thread. In addition to these basic functionalities, more features will be developed in the future.

autodone replaces autoChirp, which offered the same functionality for Twitter before the Twitter API and Twitter itself was massively restricted regarding free and ethical usage.
